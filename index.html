Risk Management Framework

Cultural asset investment carries unique risks including authenticity concerns, physical damage, market illiquidity, and regulatory uncertainty. Our comprehensive risk mitigation strategy addresses each vector:

  • Authenticity Risk: Multi-layered authentication combining forensic science, AI-powered brushstroke analysis, and consensus opinions from minimum three recognized experts per asset
  • Physical Risk: All assets stored in Tier IV+ climate-controlled facilities with 24/7 monitoring, seismic protection, and insurance coverage at 125% of appraised value
  • Market Risk: Diversification across 40+ assets, multiple categories, time periods, and geographic origins; mandatory 3-year minimum holding periods for core positions
  • Regulatory Risk: Ongoing engagement with SEC, FINRA, and international regulatory bodies; full compliance with Howey Test requirements and securities registration protocols
  • Technology Risk: Redundant blockchain infrastructure, quarterly third-party security audits, bug bounty program, and comprehensive cybersecurity insurance
